🏗️ Miami Makers Model (MMM)

Revolutionary "Partners Not Paychecks, Partners Not Investors" Development Approach

🎯 Model Introduction

The Miami Makers Model (MMM) revolutionizes real estate development by transforming traditional contractor relationships into equity partnerships.

  • Core Innovation: Transforms contractors into equity partners
  • "Partners Not Paychecks" approach: Direct stakes in developments
  • Active Participation: Partners vote on key decisions
  • SEC Compliance: Howey Test approved structure

🔄 Revolutionary Benefits

  • 2x+ efficiency from ownership motivation
  • Eliminates passive investor limitations
  • Higher quality through partner accountability
  • Scalable to developments worldwide
  • Proven success in Miami market
